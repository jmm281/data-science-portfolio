# NYC Traffic Accident Analysis (2019–2024)

## Objective
Exploratory and geospatial analysis of traffic accidents in New York City to identify patterns, risk factors, and high-risk areas.

---

## Dataset
Large dataset containing traffic accident records including:
- Date and time
- Location (latitude/longitude)
- Vehicle types involved
- Injuries and fatalities
- Contributing factors

---

## Methodology

### Data Cleaning
- Processing and cleaning large-scale real-world dataset
- Handling missing and inconsistent values

### Exploratory Data Analysis
- Temporal analysis of accidents (hour/day/year patterns)
- Identification of high-risk zones in NYC
- Analysis of vehicle types and accident causes

### Geospatial Analysis
- Mapping accident locations using latitude/longitude
- Visualization of accident density across NYC

### Visualization
- Interactive maps and charts to explore accident distribution
- Trend analysis of accident severity and frequency

---

## Technologies
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Folium, IPython

---

## Key Outcomes
- Identification of high-risk streets and zones in NYC
- Understanding of temporal patterns in accident occurrence
- Geospatial visualization of accident hotspots
